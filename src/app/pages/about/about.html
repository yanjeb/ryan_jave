About Our Church

A Christ-centered church committed to worship, discipleship, and serving our community.

Our Story

Living Temple Foursquare Gospel Church is a Christ-centered community rooted in the rich heritage of the Foursquare movement. Inspired by the vision of the International Church of the Foursquare Gospel, founded by Aimee Semple McPherson in 1923, our church continues the mission of proclaiming Jesus Christ as Savior, Baptizer, Healer, and Soon-Coming King.

As the Foursquare Church expanded into the Philippines in 1949 and later became an established national movement, Living Temple was born with a passion to bring hope, healing, and discipleship to families in our local community. Through worship services, outreach programs, youth gatherings, and leadership development, the church has become a spiritual home for generations of believers.

Today, Living Temple Foursquare Gospel Church continues to stand as a beacon of faith, committed to transforming lives through the Gospel and equipping people to live out God’s purpose.

Mission

To develop more and growing disciples and leaders together on mission, sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ, building strong families, and equipping believers for service in the Kingdom of God.

Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that transforms lives, empowers leaders, and impacts communities through worship, discipleship, evangelism, and compassionate service.

A Word from Our Pastor
